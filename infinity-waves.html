Game Features

Melee Combat — Sword
Fluid close-range combat with sword attacks, combo chains, and hit-reaction animations driven by UE5 Animation Blueprints.
Ranged Combat — Bow & Arrow
Aim, draw, and release — projectile arrows with travel arc, impact detection, and enemy stagger on headshots.
Inventory System
Pick up, store, and manage items via a full UMG inventory UI. Equip weapons, armour, and consumables on the fly.
Zombie AI — Behaviour Trees
Undead enemies driven by UE5 Behaviour Trees: idle patrol, detect player, chase, attack, and retreat states.
Boss Encounters
Multiple hand-crafted bosses, each with unique abilities, attack phases, and escalating difficulty as waves increase.
Horror Environment
Dark, fog-filled levels with dynamic lighting, atmospheric sound design, and decaying environments to maximise dread.
Endless Wave System
Wave-based spawner with configurable difficulty scaling — more enemies, tougher variants, and boss waves every 5th round.
Android Optimised
Profiled for mid-range Android — draw call budgets, LODs, texture streaming, and responsive on-screen touch controls.

Challenges & Solutions

Boss AI Phase Transitions
Problem: Boss mid-fight behaviour switching caused frame spikes
Solution: Async Behaviour Tree service + pre-cached ability refs
Result: Smooth phase change with no visible hitch
Inventory on Mobile
Problem: Touch drag-and-drop felt unreliable on small screens
Solution: Tap-to-select + confirm equip pattern instead of drag
Result: Snappy and accurate on mid-range devices
Zombie Horde Performance
Problem: 20+ simultaneous AI ticking caused FPS drops
Solution: LOD-based tick rate throttling + object pooling
Result: Stable 30–60 FPS on target Android hardware
Arrow Projectile Accuracy
Problem: Arc trajectory clipped through environment
Solution: Multi-step line trace + collision sphere sweep
Result: Consistent hit registration with no ghost passes
