Environmental Puzzles
Logic-driven puzzles integrated into the world — no UI pop-ups, everything is diegetic and immersive.

Shadow of Aldoria was built as a solo project to demonstrate full-pipeline UE5 development — from initial blockout through final lighting, gameplay scripting, and packaging.

The project showcases ownership of every layer: environment art pipeline, gameplay C++/Blueprint systems, UI, audio integration, and final performance optimisation for a polished PC experience.
