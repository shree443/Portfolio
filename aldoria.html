Game Features

Mission Progression System
Missions unlock one by one, requiring players to complete the current objective before accessing the next mission.
Inventory System
Players can collect, store, and manage items with the ability to drop items back into the world anytime.
Interaction System
Interactive gameplay mechanics allow players to collect and interact with world objects and items.
AI Ghost Enemy
Blueprint-based ghost AI featuring simple enemy behavior and player interaction mechanics.
Atmospheric Horror
Dynamic lighting, fog, sound design, and dark environments create an immersive horror experience.
First-Person Gameplay
Immersive first-person gameplay designed for exploration, tension, and cinematic horror storytelling.
